About "Passing On"

Statement about the project

Sophie Diehl started "Passing On" as a summer project in data visualization supervised by J. Nathan Matias at the MIT Media Lab in 2012. measuring and changing gender diversity in the media.

J. Nathan Matias is a grad student at the MIT Media Lab Center for Civic Media, advised by Ethan Zuckerman. Nathan collaborates on technology and communities which empower people to become more creative, effective, and informed.
